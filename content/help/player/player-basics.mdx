The player is where the actual listening happens: play and skip controls, a draggable timeline, chapter navigation, and a MiniPlayer that follows you around the app so a book never really stops. This page covers the core controls — playback speed and skip intervals have their own page.

## Play, pause, and skip

Tap the center button to play or pause. It shows a brief spinner while a chapter buffers. On either side, circular skip buttons jump backward or forward by a fixed number of seconds — 15 seconds back and 30 seconds forward by default. The number under each arrow always reflects your current interval; change it in [Speed and Skip Intervals](/help/player/speed-and-skip).

## Scrubbing the timeline

Drag the dot on the progress bar to jump to any point in the book — grab near the dot and it tracks your finger, snapping to the new position when you let go. The bar isn't empty space, either: it can show colored Ghost Markers for where your guildmates are listening, and small ticks marking Timestamp Comments pinned along the way. See [Timestamp Comments](/help/player/timestamp-comments) for what those mean and how spoiler gating decides what you can see.

## Chapters

If a book has chapter metadata, chevrons flank the chapter title so you can jump to the previous or next chapter with a tap. Tap the title itself, or the list icon in the toolbar, to open the full **Chapters** sheet — every chapter numbered, with its start time, and the one you're in highlighted.

<Screenshot src="/help/player/chapter-list.png" alt="Chapters sheet showing a numbered list of chapters with start times and the current chapter highlighted" caption="Jump to any chapter, or step to the next one." />

Below the chapter title, "Chapter X of Y" is also a toggle: tap it to switch the scrubber between tracking your progress through the whole book or just the current chapter.

## The MiniPlayer

Close the full player and the **MiniPlayer** <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" /> stays pinned above the tab bar, so listening keeps going while you browse your library or check the guild. It shows the cover, title, author, and a thin progress bar, plus a play/pause button — tap anywhere else on it to reopen the full player.

If you're in a guild, the MiniPlayer also carries two quick-action icons: one opens the comment composer, the other opens a new Topic, both anchored to whatever moment you're at. Playback pauses automatically the instant you start typing, so you never lose the exact second you meant to react to.
