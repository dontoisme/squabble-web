The Sleep Timer stops playback automatically after a chosen stretch of time, so late-night chapters don't keep running once you've drifted off. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />

## Setting the timer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the full player">
    If you're browsing elsewhere, tap the <strong>MiniPlayer</strong> to expand the book you're listening to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the moon icon">
    It sits at the end of the bottom toolbar, alongside Chapters and Bookmarks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a duration">
    Pick <strong>5, 10, 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes</strong>. If the book has chapter data, <strong>End of chapter</strong> is also available — playback stops when the current chapter ends instead of at a fixed time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Screenshot src="/help/player/sleep-timer-sheet.png" alt="Sleep Timer sheet showing Off, 5 through 60 minute options, and Cancel" caption="Pick a duration, or turn the timer off." />

Once a timer is running, the moon icon fills in and shows a live countdown. Tap it again any time to open the sheet and choose a new duration, or tap **Off** to cancel it outright.

The timer keeps counting whether Squabble is in the foreground or playing in the background with your phone locked. See [Background, Lock Screen & CarPlay](/help/player/background-carplay-lockscreen).

<Callout variant="tip">
Your listening position syncs continuously, so even if you doze off before the timer fires, the <strong>Furthest Position</strong> bookmark makes it easy to find the last part you remember. See [Bookmarks](/help/player/bookmarks) for how that works.
</Callout>
