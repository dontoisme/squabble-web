Squabble quietly tracks what you read, how you read it, and how you show up for your guild — then turns those milestones into achievements. Some unlock a [Title](/help/progress/titles) along the way. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />

## Categories

Every achievement belongs to one of four categories:

- **Listening** — hours logged, books completed, playback speed, and late-night sessions.
- **Social** — comments posted, discussion topics started, and guilds joined.
- **Exploration** — rereading books with [New Read+](/help/guilds/rereads-new-read-plus).
- **Dedication** — streaks, quest wins, and long-haul commitment to your guild.

## A few examples

Achievements range from a first nudge to genuine long-haul grinds:

- **First Steps** — complete your first audiobook.
- **Century Club** — listen for 100 total hours.
- **Time Bender** — rack up 100 hours at 1.5x speed or faster.
- **Nocturnal** — listen for 10 hours after midnight.
- **Weekly Warrior** — listen every day for 7 days straight.

Each achievement has a rarity tier — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, or Legendary — reflected in its badge color.

<Callout variant="tip" title="Some achievements stay hidden">
Not every achievement is listed in advance. A handful stay secret until you stumble into them, revealing their name and description only once you've earned them.
</Callout>

## How achievements unlock

Most achievements track a number in the background as you use Squabble — books completed, comments posted, hours listened — and unlock automatically the moment you cross the target. There's nothing to claim and no form to fill out.

Some achievements also grant a Title the instant they unlock. If one does, you'll be able to equip it from **Settings** right away. Since many achievements grant a Title, the Title selector in Settings doubles as a preview: it lists every Title you've unlocked alongside the locked ones, with the requirement needed to earn each.
