Titles are honorifics you unlock by reading, discussing, and rereading — things like Bookworm or The Archivist. You can equip one at a time, and it follows you around the guild. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />

## Earning a Title

Most Titles are tied to an [achievement](/help/progress/achievements) — clear a milestone like completing 10 books, and the matching Title unlocks alongside it. A separate ladder of Titles comes from rereading: finishing a book a second, third, fourth, or fifth time through [New Read+](/help/guilds/rereads-new-read-plus) unlocks Second Scroll, Thrice-Told, Epic Rereader, and Legend-Bound in turn, and holding rereads across 3 different books earns The Archivist.

A few Titles, like Founding Member, are granted for special reasons rather than an achievement.

## Rarity tiers

Every Title carries a rarity tier, shown as a color on its badge:

- **Common** (copper) — early Titles like Novice Reader and Guild Initiate.
- **Uncommon** (green) — Bookworm, Night Owl, Second Scroll.
- **Rare** (blue) — The Delver, Social Butterfly, The Archivist.
- **Epic** (purple) — Loremaster, Guild Champion, Epic Rereader.
- **Legendary** (orange) — Grandmaster, Completionist, Legend-Bound.

## Equipping a Title

Head to **Settings** to see every Title you've unlocked and choose which one to wear. Only one Title can be equipped at a time, and switching takes effect immediately. Full steps are on the [profile settings](/help/settings/profile) page.

## Where Titles show

Your equipped Title appears:

- Below your display name in your guild's member roster.
- On your profile card.
- Next to your **Ghost Marker** when a guildmate taps it during playback.

<Callout variant="info">
Unequipping works the same way — open the Title selector in Settings and scroll past your unlocked Titles to go without one.
</Callout>
