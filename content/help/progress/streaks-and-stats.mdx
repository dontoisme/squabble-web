Squabble keeps a running tally of how you listen and how you show up for your guild. This page covers what gets counted and how streaks work. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />

## What Squabble tracks

Behind the scenes, your account keeps a running count of:

- Total hours listened
- Books completed
- Comments posted
- Discussion topics started
- Your current streak (consecutive days with listening activity)
- Average playback speed, plus hours logged at 1.5x speed or faster
- Hours listened after midnight
- Quest wins — the number of times you've been first in your guild to finish a book

These numbers are what quietly power your [achievements](/help/progress/achievements) — cross a threshold, and the matching achievement unlocks on its own.

## Streaks

A streak counts consecutive days you've listened to at least a little. Keep it going and you'll pick up two achievements along the way: **Weekly Warrior** at 7 days, and **Monthly Master** at 30 days. Miss a day and the streak resets, so a steady daily habit — even just a chapter before bed — matters more than marathon binges.

Streaks track listening activity only. Posting comments, browsing your library, or chatting in the Tavern won't keep one alive on their own — you need to actually hit play.

## Where to see them

These stats don't live on one dashboard today. Instead, they surface where they matter: your streak shows up as the Weekly Warrior and Monthly Master achievements unlock, and quest wins appear as a **First to Finish** badge in your guild's [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) once a book wraps up.

<Callout variant="tip">
Speed and night-owl habits aren't just trivia — they're tracked toward their own achievements, so that 2am chapter at 2x speed is quietly doing double duty.
</Callout>
