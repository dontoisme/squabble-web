Finishing a book in Squabble is its own small event — a celebration screen, a stamp on your record, and sometimes a new [Title](/help/progress/titles). Here's what triggers it and how to mark a book done yourself. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />

## The completion celebration

Squabble automatically celebrates a finished book once you're about 95% of the way through — or via a notification if you've stepped away from the app right as you cross that line.

- On a first completion, the celebration screen shows a trophy.
- On a reread, it shows a tier-colored circular stamp with your run number, and a headline like "Rare Read Achieved" along with "Your third run of this book."
- A run ledger strip of tier-colored dots gives you a quick look at every prior run of that book.
- Reaching a legendary-tier run or higher adds an animated glow to the whole screen.

Tap the button at the bottom to lock in the completion — it reads **Mark Quest Complete** the first time through, or **Claim Rare Status** (naming whichever tier you just reached) on a reread. Finishing a book also posts a milestone to your guild, so your Adventurers see it too.

## Marking a book complete manually

If you finished a book without Squabble catching it automatically — say, you listened to the last stretch outside the app — open the book's details and tap **Mark as Complete**. This works the same whether it's a shared guild Quest or a personal [Solo Quest](/help/guilds/solo-quests) book.

## Reread tier stamps

Every time you finish a book again through [New Read+](/help/guilds/rereads-new-read-plus), the stamp on your celebration screen moves up the loot-tier ladder — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and beyond. Hitting certain run counts also unlocks a matching reread Title, like Second Scroll on your second run or The Archivist once you've held rereads across 3 different books.

## Quest wins

If you're the first Adventurer in your guild to finish a shared Quest, you're crowned with a **First to Finish** badge, visible on the guild's Quest Board once the book wraps up. Do that 3 times and you'll unlock the Quest Champion achievement, along with its Guild Champion Title.

<Callout variant="spoiler">
Finishing a book doesn't reveal anyone else's comments early — your guildmates' Timestamp Comments still stay hidden from you until you've actually reached those moments yourself.
</Callout>
