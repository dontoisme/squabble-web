If the [FAQ](/help/troubleshooting/faq) and the rest of this section didn't solve it, here's how to get a real answer from the team.

## Reach out through your invite channel

Squabble is currently invite-only, so the fastest way to reach us is through whichever channel got you your invite or beta access in the first place — reply there with what you're seeing. Include your guild name and the book involved if the issue is guild- or playback-specific; it helps us reproduce it faster.

## Export your diagnostic logs first

Before you reach out, exporting a diagnostics log gives the team far more to work with than a description alone. Squabble's **Diagnostics** section in Settings captures activity in memory — nothing is collected unless you turn it on.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings and enable logging">
    Scroll to the <strong>Diagnostics</strong> section and turn on <strong>Enable Logging</strong>. It's off by default and doesn't run unless you flip it on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on the categories that match your issue">
    Options include Firebase calls, Battery / player, Comment reveals, Progress sync, and UI load times. Enable whichever relates to what you're seeing — enable more than one if you're not sure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reproduce the problem">
    Tap <strong>Start Capture</strong>, do the thing that triggers the issue, then tap <strong>Stop Capture</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and share">
    Tap <strong>Export Logs</strong> to open the share sheet, then send the exported file along with your report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="tip">
Turn <strong>Enable Logging</strong> back off when you're done. It's harmless left on, but off is the default for a reason.
</Callout>

## What to include

- What you expected to happen, and what happened instead
- The guild, book, or screen involved, if relevant
- Your device type and OS version
- The exported log file from the steps above
