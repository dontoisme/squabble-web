Not every book stays on your active shelf forever. This page covers swipe actions, archiving, restoring, bulk cleanup, and permanent deletion.

## Swipe actions

Both card and list layouts support the same two swipe gestures:

- **Swipe left** reveals **Archive**.
- **Swipe right** reveals **Add to Guild** (hidden if the book's already on your [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board), or if you don't have a guild yet).

## Archiving a book

Archiving is Squabble's soft-remove: it stops playback if that book is currently loaded, deletes the audio file from your device, and sets the book aside — but keeps your progress, comments, and run history intact.

You can archive a book from a swipe, from the **Archive from Library** button on its [details screen](/help/library/book-details), or in bulk (below). Either way, Squabble asks you to confirm first.

<Callout variant="warning" title="What archiving does and doesn't do">
Archiving deletes the local audio file, but your progress and comments stay in your archive — you can restore the book anytime. It's not the same as permanent deletion.
</Callout>

## Restoring an archived book

Tap the **Archived** filter chip to see everything you've set aside. Open a book from that list and tap **Re-import audio file** (or **Re-download from Audiobookshelf** for a book that came from a server) — this both restores the book to your active library and starts pulling its audio back onto your device.

## Bulk archiving

When several books are missing their audio at once — most often after a reinstall — Squabble lets you clear them out in one pass instead of archiving one at a time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Not on Device filter">
    Tap the <strong>Not on Device</strong> chip, or the <strong>Select books to archive</strong> link on the full-library recovery screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Select">
    This switches the list into selectable rows with checkboxes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose books and confirm">
    Tap each book you want gone, or <strong>Select All</strong>, then tap <strong>Archive N books</strong> at the bottom to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a book permanently

Permanent deletion is a second step, reachable only on a book that's already archived — open it and tap **Delete permanently**. This removes the book, your progress, and your run history from every device. Your guild's comments and Topics on that book stay with the guild.

<Callout variant="warning" title="This can't be undone">
Unlike archiving, permanent deletion can't be reversed. If you're not sure, archive the book instead — you can always come back and delete it later.
</Callout>
