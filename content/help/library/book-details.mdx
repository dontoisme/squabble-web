Tap any book in your library — from a card, a list row, or a folder — to open its details screen. This is the hub for everything about that one audiobook.

## Playing and resuming

The main button plays the book, or shows **Resume** once you've made progress, with a bar tracking how much you've listened and how much remains. If the book's audio isn't on your device yet, this button becomes **Re-import** or **Re-download** instead — see [downloads and missing books](/help/library/downloads-and-missing-books) for what that means.

If the book belongs to a series, a pill above the progress bar (like "The Kingkiller Chronicle #1") opens a browser for the rest of that series.

## Comments and topics

If the book is on your [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) or shared as a Solo Quest, two mini-cards show your **Comments** and **Topics** counts and open the full lists when tapped.

<Callout variant="spoiler">
Before you finish the book, these counts only include comments and topics unlocked at your current position — the rest stay hidden until you catch up. Once you mark the book complete, everything becomes visible.
</Callout>

## Marking a quest complete

Tap **Mark as Complete** when you finish a book — it flips to **Quest Complete!** and reveals every comment and topic on that book, spoilers included. Untoggling it hides them again.

Once complete, a **Start New Read+** button appears if you want to read the book again. Each fresh run through earns its own tiered loot color; see [rereads and New Read+](/help/guilds/rereads-new-read-plus) for how the tier ladder works.

## Guild status

If the book isn't in your guild yet, tap **Post to the Quest Board** to add it. If it's already there, the same spot shows **Remove from Guild** instead.

<Callout variant="warning">
Removing a book from your guild takes it off the shared Quest Board, but your own library copy, progress, and comments stay put.
</Callout>

For a personal book you'd rather not add to the shared board, a **Share with Guild** toggle turns it into a Solo Quest instead — guildmates who separately own the same book can see your comments once they reach that point, without the book cluttering the group's Quest Board.

## Housekeeping

Near the bottom of the screen you'll find **Import Extras** (re-runs metadata extraction for a book missing chapters or artwork) and the option to archive or delete the book entirely. Both are covered in [managing your books](/help/library/managing-books).
