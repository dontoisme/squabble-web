Squabble can notify you about guild activity and comment discoveries. This page covers what types of notifications you can enable or disable, and how to customize your experience.

## Comment discovery notifications

When you reach a timestamp where a guildmate has left a comment, Squabble can alert you in three ways:

### Reveal toast

A small card appears on the player showing which guildmate commented and a preview of their reaction. This is always available; you can toggle it on or off.

### Haptic feedback

When a comment reveals, your phone vibrates. This pairs with the reveal toast to give tactile confirmation. Toggle this separately from the toast.

### Background reveal notifications

If you're listening with the app closed (or in another tab), Squabble sends a system notification to your device when you cross a hidden comment. This lets you know something was waiting for you, even during background listening. Toggle this independently of the other two.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the main tab bar, tap **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notifications">
    Scroll to the **Notifications** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle comment discovery settings">
    You'll see three toggles: <strong>Comment Reveal Toast</strong>, <strong>Reveal Haptic</strong>, and <strong>Background Reveal Notifications</strong>. Turn any of them on or off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Guild activity notifications

Below comment discovery, you can enable or disable push notifications for specific guild events. Toggling any of these on means you'll receive a system notification when that event happens.

### Activity types

- **New Comments** — A guildmate comments on a book your guild is reading
- **Comment Replies** — Someone replies to your comment
- **New Topics** — A guildmate starts a new Tavern Talk discussion on a quest
- **Topic Replies** — Someone replies in a topic you're following
- **New Quests** — A guild leader adds a book to the Quest Board
- **Member Milestones** — A guildmate completes a book or reaches a reread tier
- **Guildmate Passed You** — Another Adventurer surpasses your progress in a shared book
- **Tavern Chat** — New messages in the Tavern (guild-wide free-form chat)

<Callout variant="info">
  Each notification type is independent. You might want New Quests and Milestones on (major events) but Topic Replies off (more frequent). Adjust to match your preference.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="In Notifications, find Guild Activity">
    Below the comment discovery toggles is the list of activity types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle each activity on or off">
    Green means enabled, gray means you won't be notified for that event type.
  </Step>
</Steps>
