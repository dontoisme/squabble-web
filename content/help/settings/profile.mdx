Your profile settings let you customize how other Adventurers see you in your guild and manage your account info. You can change your display name, check your email, and select which Title you're wearing.

## Editing your display name

Your display name is how guildmates see you in comments, activity feeds, and guild rosters.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the main tab bar, tap **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap your current display name">
    The name at the top of the screen is tappable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and save">
    Type your new name and tap **Save**. Your guildmates will see the change immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info">
  Display names can be 1–20 characters. Spaces and most characters are allowed.
</Callout>

## Viewing your email

Your email appears below your display name in Settings. This is the email address tied to your Squabble account.

<Callout variant="info">
  To change your email, sign out and create a new account with a different email, then rejoin your guilds using your invite code.
</Callout>

## Equipping a Title

[Titles](/help/progress/titles) are earned honorifics you've unlocked by completing books and reaching reread milestones. You can equip one Title at a time to show it next to your display name throughout the guild.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scroll down to Titles">
    In Settings, below your email is the **Titles** section showing your currently equipped Title (if any).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Title selector">
    Tap **Equip a Title** or your current Title to open the selector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Title from the list">
    Scroll through your earned Titles and select one. The modal closes and your new Title takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info">
  Unequipping a Title is the same flow — open the selector and choose a different one, or scroll past all Titles to deselect.
</Callout>
