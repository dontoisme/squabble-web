This section covers account settings, guild management, theme selection, and app info — everything you need to customize your Squabble experience and manage your account.

## Switching or leaving your guild

Your current guild appears in Settings. You can switch to a different guild you're in, join a new one, or leave your current guild entirely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the main tab bar, tap **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Guilds">
    Scroll to the **Guilds** section, which shows your current guild name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap your guild">
    This opens a sheet showing all the guilds you're a member of.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a different guild to switch">
    Tapping another guild in the list switches you to it. Your settings remain yours, but you'll now see that guild's books and activity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Leaving a guild

To leave a guild, long-press its name in the guild list. A confirmation appears — tap **Leave** to remove yourself from the guild. You'll no longer see that guild's books or comments.

<Callout variant="warning">
  Leaving a guild is permanent for you (though you can rejoin if someone invites you again). Any comments you left in that guild remain there for other members to see.
</Callout>

### Joining a new guild

If you have an invite code from a friend, use the **Join Guild** button (in the guild selection sheet or the Guild tab) to enter a 6-character code and join.

<Callout variant="info">
  You can be in multiple guilds at once and switch between them via Settings. Each guild has its own reading list and activity.
</Callout>

## Appearance and theme

Squabble respects your device's theme preference, but you can override it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="In Settings, find Appearance">
    Below Guilds is the **Appearance** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your theme">
    Options are <strong>Light</strong>, <strong>Dark</strong>, and <strong>System</strong> (which follows your device settings).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing out

When you sign out, you'll need to log in again on your next visit. Your account and all your books remain safe in Squabble — signing out just clears the app's local session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="In Settings, scroll to the bottom">
    Find the **Account** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Sign Out">
    A confirmation appears. Tap **Sign Out** again to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning">
  Signing out clears your local playback position and bookmarks — but when you log back in, your position syncs back from Squabble's cloud, so you don't lose your place.
</Callout>

## About

The **About** section at the bottom of Settings shows your current Squabble version number and build ID. This is useful for troubleshooting or confirming you're on the latest version.
