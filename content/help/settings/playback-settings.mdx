Playback settings let you configure how the player starts each book. You can set a default speed (applied when you open the player) and choose how far forward or backward you skip when you use the skip buttons.

## Setting default playback speed

The default speed applies every time you open the player to start listening to a book. You can always change the speed mid-listen without affecting this default.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the main tab bar, tap **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Playback settings">
    Scroll down to the **Playback** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Default Speed">
    Select a speed from 0.5x (half speed) up to 3.0x, in preset steps: 0.5x, 0.75x, 1.0x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2.0x, 2.5x, or 3.0x.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="tip">
  The default is 1.0x. If you're a fast listener or digest long books over many days, 1.25x saves time without feeling rushed.
</Callout>

## Configuring skip intervals

The skip buttons in the player let you jump forward or backward by a fixed amount. This setting controls how many seconds you skip.

<Steps>
  <Step title="In Settings, find Playback">
    Below Default Speed is the skip interval section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select skip backward and skip forward intervals">
    Choose your preferred interval: 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 45, 60, or 90 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info">
  The skip intervals let you catch a missed line or jump ahead if you get distracted. 15 seconds forward and backward is a solid default.
</Callout>
