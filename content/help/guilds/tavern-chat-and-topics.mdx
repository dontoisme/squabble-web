Your [guild](/help/guilds/guilds-overview) has two ways to talk, and the difference is entirely about spoilers. The **Tavern** is open, free-form chat with no gating. **Topics** are discussion threads anchored to a moment in a book that unlock only as guildmates catch up. Knowing which is which keeps you from spoiling anyone.

## The Tavern: free-form, no gating

The **Tavern** is your guild's hangout channel — real-time chat where every message is visible to every Adventurer, immediately. Each member's messages carry their avatar color. There's no timestamp tie and no progress gate: "no rules, just respect."

Find guild-wide chat on the **Tavern** tab. There's also a **per-book Tavern**, reached from a quest's detail view under **Tavern Talk**, for chatter scoped to one specific book.

<Callout variant="warning">
The Tavern has <strong>no spoiler protection</strong>. Anything you post is visible to the whole guild right away, no matter where anyone is in a book. Keep plot reveals and reactions to key moments in Topics or [timestamp comments](/help/player/timestamp-comments) instead — those are gated by progress.
</Callout>

## Topics: spoiler-gated discussion threads

A **Topic** is a discussion thread pinned to a specific moment in a book. When you create one, Squabble captures your current position as its unlock point. That Topic — and the replies on it — stay hidden from a guildmate until that guildmate has listened past the same point.

Topics are also surfaced as **Tavern Talk** on a quest's detail view, so the phrase you see in the app depends on where you are, but it's the same feature.

## Starting a Topic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reach the moment you want to discuss">
    While listening, get to the point in the book your topic is about — Squabble uses your current position as the unlock point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Topics">
    From the player, or from a quest's Tavern Talk section, tap <strong>+</strong> or <strong>Start a Topic</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write it">
    Give the Topic a title, add an optional description, and post. Guildmates behind that point won't see it until they catch up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="spoiler">
Topics unlock by progress, one guildmate at a time. In a book's Topics list, threads a guildmate hasn't unlocked yet are hidden, and the [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) hints at how many "topics waiting" a book has — content you can't see yet, waiting for you to read further. Furthest Position is the anchor: it's the furthest point you've reached, and it decides what's unlocked.
</Callout>

## Which one should you use?

- **Tavern** — logistics, jokes, "what should we read next," reactions safe for everyone. No spoilers, ever.
- **Topics** — anything tied to what actually happens in the book. The gate does the work of keeping it spoiler-safe.
