A **Guild** is your private reading party: up to 6 Adventurers who listen to audiobooks together and talk about them without spoiling anything. The guild is the social heart of Squabble — everything about reading with other people lives here.

## What a guild is

A guild is a small, invite-only group. There are no public guilds and no discovery feed — you join one because a friend handed you a 6-character invite code. Every guild holds up to 6 members, and each member is called an **Adventurer**. You can belong to more than one guild at a time and switch between them.

To start or join one, see [Creating or joining a guild](/help/guilds/create-or-join).

## The four tabs

Open the **Guild** tab and you land on one of four sub-tabs:

- **Activity** — the guild's shared feed of comments, new topics, replies, and milestones, with emoji reactions. See [the activity feed](/help/guilds/activity-feed-and-reactions).
- **Quests** — the [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board): the books your guild is reading, with completed quests tucked into their own collapsible list.
- **Adventurers** — the member roster, with each Adventurer's role, avatar color, and earned Titles.
- **Tavern** — free-form guild chat with no spoiler gating. See [Tavern chat and Topics](/help/guilds/tavern-chat-and-topics).

## What you do together

Once you have a guild, reading stops being a solo activity:

- **Take on Quests.** Add a book to the [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) and everyone can read it on their own schedule, leaving breadcrumbs for those still catching up.
- **Track a whole series.** Group a multi-book saga into a single [Epic Quest](/help/guilds/epic-quests).
- **Share personal reads.** Turn on a [Solo Quest](/help/guilds/solo-quests) to share your notes on a book that isn't on the board, with guildmates who own it too.
- **Talk without spoilers.** Leave [timestamp comments](/help/player/timestamp-comments) and start Topics that stay hidden until a guildmate reaches the same point.

<Callout variant="spoiler">
Almost everything a guild shares is spoiler-safe. Comments and Topics unlock for a guildmate only once that guildmate has listened past the moment they're pinned to. The Tavern is the one exception — it has no spoiler gating, so keep big reveals out of it.
</Callout>

## Roles

Every guild has one **owner** (shown as Guild Master) — the Adventurer who created it — and the rest are members. The owner can pin a spotlight quest to the top of the board and manage the guild. Any Adventurer can add books, leave comments, start Topics, and invite new members up to the 6-person cap.

<Callout variant="info">
The web app can show your guild's feed, invites, and notes, so you can keep up from a browser. The mobile app is where you do most of your reading and social activity. <PlatformBadge platform="web" />
</Callout>
