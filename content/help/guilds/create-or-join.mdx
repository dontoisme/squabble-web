Every [guild](/help/guilds/guilds-overview) starts with someone founding it and handing out an invite. This page covers creating a guild, joining one, belonging to several at once, and leaving.

## Creating a guild

If you don't have a guild yet, the **Guild** tab shows an empty state with the option to found one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Guild tab">
    Tap <strong>Guild</strong> in the bottom navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a guild">
    Tap <strong>Create Guild</strong>, give your guild a name, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the invite code">
    Squabble generates a 6-character invite code. Share it with the people you want to read with.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Whoever creates the guild becomes its owner, shown as **Guild Master** on the roster.

## Joining with an invite code

A guild holds up to 6 Adventurers, so you need a code from someone already inside.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Guild tab">
    Tap <strong>Guild</strong>, then choose <strong>Join Guild</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your invite code">
    Type the 6-character code exactly as it was shared. Codes skip easily confused characters, so there's no 0, O, I, 1, or L to trip over.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join">
    Tap <strong>Join</strong>. You'll land in the guild's Activity feed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Joining from a web link

Invites also travel as links. Opening a link shaped like `squabbleinn.com/invite/ABC234` in a browser takes you to a join page for that guild. <PlatformBadge platform="web" /> The 6-character code at the end of the link is the same code you'd type into the app, so either path lands you in the same guild.

<Callout variant="info" title="Inviting others">
From the <strong>Adventurers</strong> tab, use <strong>Summon Allies</strong> to share your guild's invite code or link again. Anyone can invite, up to the 6-member cap.
</Callout>

## Belonging to more than one guild

You can be an Adventurer in several guilds at once — a series club with one group of friends, a horror-only guild with another. Each guild keeps its own Quest Board, Activity feed, Tavern, and roster; nothing bleeds between them.

To switch, open the **Guild** tab and choose a different guild from the guild switcher at the top. Your progress in a book is yours and follows you, but comments and Topics you write are posted to whichever guild you're currently in.

## Leaving a guild

You can leave a guild from the **Adventurers** tab.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Adventurers tab">
    In the guild you want to leave, tap <strong>Adventurers</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Leave">
    Scroll down and tap <strong>Leave Guild</strong>, then confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning">
Leaving is permanent — you'll lose access to that guild's Quest Board, Tavern, and feed, and you'd need a fresh invite to rejoin. If you're the <strong>owner</strong>, you can't leave until you transfer ownership to another Adventurer first; do that from <strong>Settings → Account and guild management</strong>.
</Callout>
