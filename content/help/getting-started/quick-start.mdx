Already have an account? Here's the fastest path from a fresh install to your first shared listen: import a book, press play, and join a Guild — about 5 min total.

<Callout variant="info">
No account yet? Start with [Creating your account](/help/getting-started/creating-your-account) first — it takes less than a minute.
</Callout>

## Import a book (about 1 min)

Open your Library tab and tap **Add from phone**, then choose an audiobook file from your device. Squabble adds it to your library once the import finishes. See [Importing Books](/help/library/importing-books) for supported formats, or connect an [Audiobookshelf](/help/audiobookshelf/connecting-your-server) server instead if you keep your audiobooks on a self-hosted library.

## Press play (about 1 min)

Tap the book in your Library to open its details, then tap **Play**. Playback opens in the full player, with chapters, a Sleep Timer, and speed control all one tap away. Leave a [timestamp comment](/help/player/timestamp-comments) whenever something's worth flagging — it stays hidden from guildmates until they catch up to that point.

## Join or start a Guild (about 2-3 min)

Head to the Guild tab. If a friend already sent you a 6-character invite code, tap **Join with a code** and enter it. Starting fresh instead? Tap **Forge a guild**, name it, and Squabble generates an invite code you can share right away. Either path is covered step by step in the [onboarding tour](/help/getting-started/onboarding-tour).

<Screenshot src="/help/getting-started/ftue_07_complete.png" alt="Guild activity screen showing the Guild name, 1 of 6 adventurers, and Activity, Quests, and Adventurers tabs" caption="You've arrived — your Guild's home base." />

## You're set

That's the whole loop: a book in your library, playback underway, and a Guild to share it with. From here, your comments start unlocking for guildmates as they reach the same point in the book, and the [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) keeps everyone's progress in view.
