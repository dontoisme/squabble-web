The first time you sign in, Squabble walks you through three stops before dropping you into the app: find your Guild, add a book, and (optionally) connect a self-hosted server. This page covers what to expect at each one — you can also skip straight to the [quick start](/help/getting-started/quick-start) if you'd rather move fast.

## Welcome — find your fellowship

After you [create your account](/help/getting-started/creating-your-account), you land on a welcome screen with two paths and a skip link.

<Screenshot src="/help/getting-started/ftue_03_welcome.png" alt="Find your fellowship screen with Forge a guild and Join with a code buttons, plus a Do this later skip link" caption="Every new Adventurer picks a path here." />

- **Forge a guild** — start a brand-new Guild and become its founder.
- **Join with a code** — enter a 6-character invite code a friend already sent you.
- **Do this later** — skip both for now and go straight into the app. You can join or create a Guild anytime from the Guild tab.

## Joining a Guild with a code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Join with a code">
    This opens a full-screen entry form for your invite code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your 6-character invite code">
    Type it exactly as your friend sent it — Squabble capitalizes it automatically as you type. Inside the app this field is labeled the <strong>Guild Rune</strong>, but it's the same invite code described on the [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Join">
    You're in — Squabble takes you on to the add-a-book step so you can grab whatever your new guildmates are reading. Check the <strong>Quests</strong> tab afterward to see the guild's [Quest Board](/help/guilds/quest-board).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a Guild

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Forge a guild">
    Squabble opens a form for your new Guild's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your Guild">
    Type anything from a series title to an inside joke — up to 30 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Forge Guild">
    Squabble creates the Guild and immediately generates your invite code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Screenshot src="/help/getting-started/ftue_05_guild_created.png" alt="Guild Forged success screen showing a 6-character invite code, a Share code button, a Next — Add a book button, and a Skip for now link" caption="Your Guild Rune, ready to send to friends." />

Tap **Share code** to send it through any app on your phone, or just read it off and tell someone. From here, tap **Next — Add a book** to continue the tour, or **Skip for now** to head into the app — you can invite Adventurers and pin a book from the Guild tab later.

<Callout variant="tip">
You aren't limited to one Guild. You can join or create additional Guilds later from the Guild tab if you want to read with more than one group.
</Callout>

## Adding your first book

The last stop offers two ways to stock your shelves.

<Screenshot src="/help/getting-started/ftue_06_book_add.png" alt="Welcome traveler screen with Sync Audiobookshelf and Add from phone buttons" caption="Pick a quest before you enter the library." />

- **Sync Audiobookshelf** — connect a self-hosted [Audiobookshelf](/help/audiobookshelf/connecting-your-server) server and pull your library from there. <PlatformBadge platform="mobile" />
- **Add from phone** — import an audiobook file straight from your device. See [Importing Books](/help/library/importing-books) for supported formats.

Tap **Enter the library** at any point to skip this step with nothing added yet — you can import a book anytime. When the tour ends, Squabble drops you on the **Guild** tab, your new home base.

## Skipping ahead

Nothing in this tour is required in one sitting — the welcome screen's **Do this later**, the guild-created screen's **Skip for now**, and **Enter the library** each let you bail out early, and the join/create forms have a **Back** link to retrace a step. If you already belong to a Guild — for example, you signed up on a second device — Squabble skips the tour automatically and takes you straight into the app.
