You need a Squabble account to save your library, join a Guild, and keep your progress in sync. This page covers every way to sign up, sign back in, and recover a forgotten password.

## Signing up with email

From the Inn's front door, tap **First time here** to open the sign-up form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type the email address you want your account tied to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a password">
    The form labels this field <strong>Passphrase</strong> — it's just your password, at least 6 characters. Enter it again in <strong>Confirm Passphrase</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Claim my seat">
    This creates your account and drops you into the [onboarding tour](/help/getting-started/onboarding-tour).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Screenshot src="/help/getting-started/ftue_02_signup_form.png" alt="Squabble sign-up form with email and passphrase fields, a Claim my seat button, and Continue with Google and Continue with Apple buttons below it" caption="Sign the ledger — or skip it with Google or Apple." />

By signing up, you agree to Squabble's Terms & Code of Conduct.

## Signing up or signing in with Google or Apple

Tap **Continue with Google** on either the sign-up or sign-in screen to use your Google account instead of a password — no Passphrase to create or remember.

On iPhone and iPad, **Continue with Apple** is also available beneath the Google button. Apple sign-in only works on Apple devices, so Android phones and tablets won't show it.

<Callout variant="info">
On the web app, sign-up and sign-in currently use email and password only — Google and Apple continue buttons are mobile-only for now.
</Callout>

## Logging back in

From the landing screen, tap **I've been here before** instead of **First time here**. Enter your email and Passphrase and tap **Come inside**, or use the same Google or Apple button you signed up with.

## Forgot your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Forgot your passphrase?">
    This link sits just below the password field on the sign-in screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Your email is pre-filled if you already typed it. Tap <strong>Send reset link</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    A "Raven dispatched" screen confirms the email is on its way. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info" title="Signed up with Google or Apple?">
There's no Passphrase to reset — sign in with the same Google or Apple account you originally used instead.
</Callout>

The password reset link currently lives on the mobile sign-in screen only. If you created your account on the web app and need to reset your password, use the mobile app's **Forgot your passphrase?** link — it works against the same account either way.
